Corporate Overview

Headquartered in Bangalore, AiPlex Software Pvt. Ltd. was founded in 2003 as an IT Services Company. In 2008, the company identified a dearth in the Content Protection Domain and introduced Online Antipiracy and Brand Protection Solutions to Bollywood. We achieved a market share of more than 85% of theatrical releases of Bollywood and Regional films in the Indian subcontinent.

As we went global in pursuit of a wider clientele, we established ourselves as a trusted Antipiracy partner to European and American Media & Entertainment Companies. AiPlex International S.A. in Brussels (Belgium) was incorporated in 2011 to service our global media clients especially in North and South America, Europe and the Middle East.

We evolved from a small team to a team of over 250+ full-time executives. It is a matter of pride that we have a client retention rate of 98% and that our clients continue to be delighted by our services. We add a new client or piece of content almost every day.
